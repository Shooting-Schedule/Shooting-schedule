## **🚀 What is a Shooting Schedule?**

A shooting schedule is a **production roadmap** that organizes:

* Scenes to be shot  
* Locations  
* Cast availability  
* Shoot dates & timelines

Studiovity simplifies this complex process with **AI-driven automation and smart scheduling tools**.
